From today's featured article

The Mass Arcane Web (M.A.W.) is a revolutionary ethereal network created by Archmage Aceta Minoph in 1955 A.U. that allows instant transmission of information across vast distances. Using a combination of divination magic and mana-crystal relay stations, the M.A.W. connects millions of users throughout Ohsa and beyond. The network is accessed through Communication Scrolls - enchanted slabs that can display text, images, and even limited moving pictures...
